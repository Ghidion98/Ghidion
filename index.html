Who We Are

Founded in 1939, Asmara Brewery is renowned for producing a range of beverages, including its flagship Asmara beer.

The company also manufactures spirits like gin, cognac, and the anise-flavored Ariki. Beyond its beverage production, Asmara Brewery contributes to local sports by operating a football team that competes in the Eritrean Premier League and in FIFA competitions.
